Who We Are

Prakash L. Parmar (CEO)

Our Business was stated at small village of Amereli district named SavarKundla in Gujarat state. The Business was started by the name of "Jarawala Traders" in 1995. Now in 2021 the Company has changed its name to "MaaKrupa Hardware & Tools" and the location of company had been changed to Surat, Gujarat, India.

We sells every kinds of Tools such as :
Hand Tools, Power Tools, Power Tools Spares & Accessories, Engineering Tools, Welding Rods, Welding Accessories, Gas Welding Accessories, Carpenter Tools, Abrasives, Garage Tools & Drill Bits...
